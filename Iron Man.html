IRON MAN

A wealthy American business magnate, playboy, philanthropist, inventor and ingenious scientist, Anthony Tony Stark. In a cave in Afghanistan, Tony Stark and Ho Yinsen developed the first Iron Man suit, which Stark used to escape his captors. Inventor Tony Stark applies his genius for high-tech solutions to problems as Iron Man, the armored Avenger.
